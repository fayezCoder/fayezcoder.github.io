Restaurant Menu

A digital menu app for restaurants, making ordering and browsing simple and fast.

The Restaurant Menu application is designed to provide an interactive and user-friendly digital menu for restaurants. The app allows customers to browse through various categories of food and beverages, customize orders, and view prices instantly.

  • A fully digital menu with clear categories for easy navigation.
  • Customization options for meals (e.g., toppings, sides, or special instructions).
  • Real-time price updates and availability of items.
  • Designed to work seamlessly across tablets and smartphones.

Whether you're managing a small bistro or a large restaurant, the Restaurant Menu app streamlines your ordering process and improves the overall dining experience for your customers.

Smart Menu & Invoice

A seamless ordering and invoicing experience for restaurants.

Smart Menu & Invoice is a user-friendly application that allows restaurant staff to easily manage orders and generate invoices for customers. The app features:

  • An intuitive menu display for customers to select items.
  • Automatic addition of selected items to the invoice in real-time.
  • The ability to print invoices with a single click.

Whether you are running a small café or a large restaurant, this app streamlines the ordering process and simplifies invoice management, ensuring an efficient and smooth customer experience.

ALCPT App

A comprehensive tool to enhance your English language skills.

The ALCPT (American Language Course Placement Test) application is designed to help users test and improve their English language proficiency. It contains:

  • 10 tapes, each comprising 100 questions.
  • A variety of question types to assess listening, reading, and comprehension skills.
  • An intuitive interface for a seamless testing experience.

Whether you’re preparing for exams or looking to refine your language abilities, the ALCPT app is the perfect companion to track your progress and enhance your skills.

Mnjz App

Your secure digital vault for certificates.

The Mnjz app provides a secure and convenient way for users to store, organize, and access their certificates anytime, anywhere. It ensures the safety of important documents with advanced security measures.

  • Easily upload and manage all your certificates in one place.
  • Access your documents securely from any device.
  • Share certificates effortlessly with a single click when needed.

With Mnjz, you no longer need to worry about losing or misplacing your valuable certificates. Everything you need is safely stored and accessible at your fingertips.
